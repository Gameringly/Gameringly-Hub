Sonic into Cyberspace

Sonic into Cyberspace is a Sonic Advance/Rush inspired fangame that aims to innovate and expand upon 2D Sonic gameplay with an emphasis on freedom in its movement and combat that naturally flows with the platforming

Maintian high speed to enter boost mode which will allow you to blast through enemies and reach even greater speeds, you've gotta go fast to go faster!

Sonic 2 Time Split is a fanmade sequal to Sonic CD, accurately recreating the originals time travel mechanic.

Explore the vast otherworldly stages through four time periods to find the broken pieces of the Time Stones and create a good future!
